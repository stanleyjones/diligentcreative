    Plan.

    Diligent offers strategy consulting, brainstorming facilitation, and project management to bring your site (and you) to the cutting edge.

    Build.

    A full-service creative agency, Diligent handles concepts to coding, whether it's handwritten HTML or a custom theme for your existing platform.

    Test.

    Diligent offers a wide range of diagnostics, from quantitative traffic analysis to qualitative user testing, and helps you translate them into concrete actions.

    Refine.

    Unlike most agencies, Diligent stays with you to improve your website over time. Seize opportunities as they arise, not in the next major redesign.
